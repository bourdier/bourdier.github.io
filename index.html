Welcome to my little piece of space on the Internet.

Here, I share here a few snippets about my projects and the things I love.

My name is Theo, I'm learning web development since September 2022. I'm passionate about Internet and computers since I was very young, I always liked to tinker with anything that had a printed circuit (until I designed some myself for a project called Hortus), and to design small machines.

I have a few basics in Python, which are very useful to write small scripts to automate tasks in my everyday life (such as Budgie, a script to sort photos by creating folders named after the date they were taken by importing the EXIF from the metadata).

On the hardware side, I have a Lenovo Thinkcentre M700 Tiny with an i3-6100T coupled with 24GB of DDR4 RAM transformed into a Linux server running Proxmox. This server allows me to tinker and understand the functioning of a server, I host different services such as a Minecraft server for my friends and me, Jellyfin which is a media center that allows to watch movies and series, a small NAS, and soon a temporary file uploading service that will be available on this site.
